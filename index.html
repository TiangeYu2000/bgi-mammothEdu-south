Petalglow: Let there be light

to comfort, to companion, to inspire

A Trailer of Petalglow

The Problem

Anxiety, intensified by societal pressures like neijuan, leads to restless nights. The resulting lack of rest amplifies anxiety, creating a vicious cycle. Traditional night lights disrupt melatonin production, worsening anxiety at midnight.

Our Solution

We developed a bioluminescent plant companion, Petalglow, with 150% increased light intensity compared to previous fungal bioluminescence pathways (FBP) in transgenic tobacco plants.

Daytime Solution

At daytime, Petalglow promotes relaxation through horticultural therapy. Daily tasks like watering the plant alleviate anxiety and stress, and enhance mood.

Night Solution

At night, Petalglow emits soft, calming bioluminescent light, creating an ideal pre-sleep environment without disrupting sleep-inducing darkness.
